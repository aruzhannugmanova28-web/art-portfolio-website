about

A Central Asian girl from Kazakhstan, currently making things at Milton Academy.

I work across paintings, ceramics, 3D modeling, and digital illustration. My work returns again and again to the same questions: what does it mean to belong to two places at once? How do you carry a culture across an ocean? What survives translation, and what can only be made new?

Many of my pieces draw from Kazakh visual heritage, the shanyrak, the ornaments, the steppe, and place it in conversation with the worlds I'm finding here. The star you saw at the start of this site appears throughout my work. I keep coming back to it.
